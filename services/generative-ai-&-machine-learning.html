What is Generative AI?

Generative AI is a subfield of artificial intelligence (AI) focused on creating new data, like text, images, code, or music. Generative AI models are trained on massive datasets of existing data, and they learn to identify patterns and relationships within that data. This enables them to generate entirely new and original creative text formats, like poems, code, scripts, musical pieces, email, letters, etc., never seen before but consistent with the style and content of the training data.
